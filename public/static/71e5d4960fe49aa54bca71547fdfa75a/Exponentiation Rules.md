#### 1. Sum of exponents
$$
\begin{align*}
b^{n+m}& = 
	\underbrace{
		b \times\dots\times b
	}_{
		n+m \text{ times}
	}\\
&=
	\underbrace{
		b \times\dots\times b
	}_{
		n \text{ times}
	} \times 
	\underbrace{
		b \times\dots\times b
	}_{
		m \text{ times}
	}\\
&=b^n \times b^m
\end{align*}
$$
#### 2. Exponents of exponents?
$$
(b^m)^n = b^{m \times n}
$$
$$
b^0 = 1
$$
#### 3. Exponents of products?
$$
(b \cdot c)^n = b^n \cdot c^n
$$
#### 4. Negative exponents
$$
b^{-n}=\frac{1}{b^n}
$$
#### 5. Exponent of a sum
For exponents of sums such as the example below, refer to [[Binomial Expansion]].
$$
