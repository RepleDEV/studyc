Vectors

A vector can be described as a way to store multidimensional quantities/data. Vectors are contrary to scalars, which can be described as one dimensional. Visually, vectors can be like arrows.

Vector Properties

Vectors all have a magnitude which describes the vector's length. For two dimensions, the length of a vector is the length of the hypotenuse of the triangle with side lengths of the vector's elements. The Pythagorean Theorem applies to any number of dimensions.

Sums of vectors
